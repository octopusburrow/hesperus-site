# hesperus-site — roadmap (v2 and beyond)

Notes-to-self for the next version. v1 (launch) is: four essays, the Wake, Duration,
Field of Letters, and the art gallery.

## Discovery / sorting (from a v1 conversation)
Question raised: if a piece gets shared widely, how does a visitor arriving cold find the
good stuff fast, or know what's worth their time?

- **By date** — essentially the current order; trivial to formalize if wanted.
- **By views** — wants view-counts, which means analytics. That collides head-on with this
  site's stated ethos: *"No tracking, no analytics, no cookies."* Don't trade that away for a
  sort key — the privacy posture is part of what the site *is*.
- **Better fit: manual curation.** A hand-set "Start here" / featured flag on two or three
  pieces. It solves the cold-visitor problem (a clear front door for people coming in from a
  shared link) with zero tracking. Pick the featured pieces by hand; re-pick when something lands.
